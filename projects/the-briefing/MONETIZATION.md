# Monetization — The Briefing

> Revenue models and experiments.

---

## Target Segments

### 1. General Public (Free Tier)
- Weekly digest
- Basic MP voting records
- Bill summaries

**Monetization:** Volume → sponsorships, grants

### 2. Engaged Citizens (Paid Tier — $5-10/month)
- Real-time alerts ("They're voting on this tomorrow")
- Full MP voting history
- Bill progression tracking
- Comment/discussion access

**Monetization:** Subscriptions

### 3. Property Professionals (Premium — $50-100/month)
- Real-time alerts on zoning changes
- Development pipeline tracking
- Early warning on projects
- Historical decision database

**Monetization:** B2B subscriptions

### 4. Journalists/Researchers (Pro — $100+/month)
- API access
- Data exports
- Historical archive
- Advanced search

**Monetization:** Data licensing

---

## Revenue Phases

### Phase 1: Bootstrap (Months 1-3)
- Local business sponsorship
- Tip jar / Patreon
- Grant funding (journalism/democracy initiatives)

### Phase 2: Product-Market Fit (Months 4-8)
- Launch paid tiers
- Developer/property pro tier
- Election cycle: voter guides, candidate profiles

### Phase 3: Scale (Months 9-12)
- White-label to other regions
- Data licensing
- Expand team
