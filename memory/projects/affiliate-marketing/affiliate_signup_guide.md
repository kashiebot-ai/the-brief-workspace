## What You'll Need
- **Email address** (use one you check regularly)
- **Website URL** (you can use a placeholder like "coming soon" or describe your planned site)
- **Payment method** (PayPal most common)
- **Tax information** (may be required for US-based programs)

## Signup Tips
1. **Use consistent details** across all programs
2. **Save your affiliate links** in a secure place (password manager or spreadsheet)
3. **Check email** for approval notifications (may take 24-48 hours)
4. **Bookmark your affiliate dashboards** for each program

## Next Steps After Signup
1. Update `config/wordpress.json` with your new affiliate links
2. Run the automation system to generate content with your links
3. Set up website (domain + hosting + WordPress)
4. Publish content and start tracking conversions

## Time Estimate
- **4 priority programs:** 20-30 minutes total
- **All 7 programs:** 45-60 minutes
